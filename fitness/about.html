Common Excercise

The following are common excercises that we encourage our clients to do as part of their daily taining.

Burpee
Burpees are a great, full body excercise to increase your stregth and endurance. Begin in a standing position, drop into a squat and extend your hand forward, kick your feet back and then forward again quickly, and then jump up from a squatted position
Plank
Planks build your core stregnth. To perform a plank, get in a push up position, and rest forearms on the floor. Hold the position as long as you can.
Mountain Climber
A very good cardio excercise. Place hannds on the floor in pushup position, bring one kneww up to your chest, and then switch as quickl;y as you can, as if you were climbing a mountain.
